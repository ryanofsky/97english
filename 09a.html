What is art?

My Spanish teacher told us the names of the top five paintings. There are five paintings that experts have established are better then everything else. They all agree. If you like #3 better than #2 you're wrong. Apparently, the same thing goes for literature. There are 2.5 American books that are better than all other American books. All of this is completely stupid. If  would say that Michael Crieton kills Nathaniel Hawthorne, what is wrong with that? I have great respect for good artists and writers. But, the so-called experts who sit around and analyze, making lists of plant

 imagery and ranking works, What is the point? What are they accomplishing? It is good that they can appreciate a piece of art of literature, but to pretend any of that stuff is really important or treat it as if it was some kind of exact science is pretty ridiculous.
