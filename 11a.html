God

95% of people in the world believe in a God (according the the movie, Contact). Only 60% of scientists believe in God (or was it 40%?, from a Wall Street Journal Op-Ed)

The word, 'faith' has a good connotation. I think 'faith' is terrible. How is it a good thing to believe something without question or thought?

Observations and data do not suggest the presence of a god. If there is absolutely no trace of a god then how do you say one exists. You have the burden of proof.

  

My parents say that right now I might not believe in god, but that I will when I get older.

Why would god waste his time on us?
