Before we even realized what was going on, the car had flown over the guardrail and we were in midair over the river. Thinking quickly, I took a long chain of paper clips out of my bookbag, fastened one end to a seat, and threw the other end out of the window, and up to the bridge. Luckily for us it caught some other section of the guardrail. But our troubles were not over. Although the paper clip chain was more than capable of supporting the weight of the van, the guardrail was creaking and was about to crumble. Once again, I sprung into action. I climbed up into the front of the van, shattered the windshield with my fist, and ascended up the long chain of paper clips. Once I was at the top, I took hold of the chain, and used every bit of my strength to pull the van back up to safety. I had not only saved my own life but also the lives of three others, and all thanks to paper clips.
