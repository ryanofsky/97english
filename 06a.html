Conformity

In our everybody vs. Matt Kelly discussions about conformity, we wanted him to conform to our opinion that conformity was not a good thing. Matt Kelly had the opinion that conformity was good because there is less pressure to stand out and do something original and that it makes things simpler and easier. Everybody else says that conforming means giving up individuality and identity. As usual, all I have to say is, Who Cares? If you want to conform, then go ahead an conform. If you don't, then don't, don't! It's an individual decision. I don't thing we'll ever see

  

the day when everybody conforms. And if everybody doesn't conform, not conforming will lose its fun. So if Matt Kelly wants to go against the group and say that conforming is good and everybody else wants Matt to go with the popular opinion and not conform, then that's just fine too.
