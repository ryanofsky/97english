So that night, I went right over to the Camp Hill Mall. There is this lady who hangs out there in the arcade who is some kind of drug dealer / psychic. I don’t do drugs, but I was hoping that maybe I could use her psychic abilities to recover some lost memories about paper clips. So she took me to her office, and tried out several treatments. She hypnotized me, tried some electric shock therapy, and put me in front of this strange machine with mysterious sounds and lights. Actually, I think she was just

 playing Sonic the Hedgehog, but I was unable to tell without my glasses. Anyway, none of it seemed to work. But suddenly, when she was on the phone with some sort of "unlicensed brain surgeon", the memories came flooding back, and tears came to my eyes… I will share one of these memories here.
